AI Usage Guidelines

01

Purpose & Clarity

Always be clear about your purpose when using AI tools. Define specific goals and expected outcomes before beginning.

02

Transparency

Document your AI usage. Keep records of which tools you used, how you used them, and what content was AI-generated.

03

Verification

Always verify AI-generated content. Cross-check facts, citations, and information with reliable sources.

04

Learning Focus

Use AI as a learning aid, not a replacement for understanding. Ensure you comprehend the material AI helps you with.

05

Attribution

Properly cite AI tools when required by your institution. Follow academic honesty policies and guidelines.

06

Privacy

Be mindful of data privacy. Don't share sensitive information with AI tools without understanding their data policies.
